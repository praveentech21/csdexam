Pop-Up Store

By cascade-preset-2 Admin
September 12, 2022

To celebrate the start of the spring we're hosting a pop-up in the Royal Botanical Gardens for the next 3 weeks. 

We'll be showcasing a range of new products, including our new Butterfly plant stand. What could be a more perfect way to celebrate spring?

We're also going to be working with the staff of the Botanical Gardens to to hold a series of workshops about all the different native and tropical plants they have in their collection. Great news for readers who think they're not capable of keeping their plants alive - we'll be giving you all the tips you need to keep your plants green and thriving in the coming months.
